Diving & Snorkeling Courses

NON-CERTIFICATION COURSES

Once upon a time, not too long ago, diving was a pastime for only the toughest, most rugged, ex-military type men. However with the advances in technology and our new understanding of how the human body works, this hard-core sport has evolved into a hobby which can now be pursued by even the most delicate of flowers. We offer the following one day courses for those of you who are simply looking to cross diving or snorkeling off your bucket list and for those of you who are looking to get your kids more active. These short courses are also an amazing option for families looking for an exciting and “alternative” activity which will be remembered fondly by all for a life time.



PADI DISCOVER SCUBA DIVING
A one day beginner course with two afternoon ocean dives
PADI BUBBLEMAKER
For young scuba divers who are looking for safe, action-packed fun
PADI SEAL TEAM
Young enthusiasts advance past the basics onto exciting AquaMissions
PADI SKIN DIVER
Visit the Caribbeans depths with our breath hold techniques
